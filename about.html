About Us

At Aina, we believe in the power of storytelling to spark imagination, foster learning, and bring families closer together. As a modern children's book publishing company, our mission is to create high-quality, engaging, and diverse books that captivate young minds—all while making them easily accessible through our innovative e-commerce platform.

Our Story

Aina was founded with a simple yet powerful idea: to bridge the gap between traditional publishing and modern digital convenience. Our journey began when Fiona—a passionate storyteller and advocate for children’s literacy—noticed a gap in the market. Parents, educators, and young readers needed a better way to discover and access beautifully illustrated, meaningful books without the limitations of traditional bookstores.

Determined to create a solution, we launched Aina as an online-first publishing house, allowing families to explore and purchase our books with just a few clicks. From the very beginning, we set out to redefine the publishing industry by combining the timeless magic of children’s literature with the efficiency and reach of e-commerce.
